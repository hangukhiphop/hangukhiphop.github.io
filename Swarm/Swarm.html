Swarm is a 3D action Multiplayer Online Battle Areana (MOBA).

It is currently being developed under USC Gamepipe's Advanced Game Projects (AGP).

I originated the concept, and successfully pitched it to AGP during the spring of 2015 with a team of engineers and artists. The process included a Written pitch and a Pitch presentation.

My role in the project is creative director. I design the characters, mechanics, worlds, etc. and document them. My first task was to write the Game Design Document, which summarizes the primary aspects of the game design. I constantly collaborate with engineers and artists to develop game mechanics and assets according to design. Furthermore, I iteratively ensure that the resulting gameplay is conducive to the optimal player experience.
